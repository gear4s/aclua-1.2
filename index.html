This is the home page for Assault Cube's Lua Mod (by sveark) updated to 1.2+

Who's the guy that done this?

His in-game and online nickname is Narwhaal, and he's usually on the IRC channel #graphox.us on GameSurge

Why did he do this?

Boredom, mostly. He also was tired of the lack of 1.2+ servermods, so he ported sveark's Lua mod.

Well, upgrade obviously. I plan to upgrade the Lua implementation to 5.2, and also clean up the original source. Then I plan on adding features, like, an IRC bot and webadmin interface.

Do you have any help with any of the above features, or did you when porting ?

No, I've so far done all porting/implementing myself, but I had some support from B}PhaNtom :)

Does Sveark know about this port ?

Maybe, maybe not. I've posted info about it on Assault Cube's forum, so if he's there, he knows this.
